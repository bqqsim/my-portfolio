Who is The Man of The Hour?

Hello, my name is Baasim. My birthday is on October 17, 2004 and I am a Libra! That doesn't really
matter, I was just saying that just in case anyone was wondering. I was born in Pakistan and I came
to Canada at the age of 6 in 2011. My family and I spent most of our lives in Dubai, except for my
youngest sister. She was 1 when we moved to Canada and my younger brother was 3, so they've
spent most of her life here! As a child I had 0 interest in school and I just wanted to either play with
toys, or run around and play soccer. Soccer was the first sport I fell in love with, and as
I got older I started liking basketball more!

Interests

For my interests, I really like sports. If I were to choose either video games or playing basketball
outside, I would definitely choose to go outside and play basketball. I'm not too sure why I've
grown such a strong attachment to basketball, but it is one of the things I really enjoy doing.
Another one of my interests is playing video games. There's just something about putting a
headset on and staring at the TV for hours that excites me to a different level. I usually never
play alone though. I have three of my best friends hop on with me and we play for hours.
The two games I play the most are Forza and Call of Duty Warzone. Forza is pretty cool and I like
racing games but, It's not something I play all the time. I play Warzone with my friends a lot though.
I can talk for hours about my interests but lastly, I am going to talk about my interest in food. I think
food is a really cool thing.I don't cook, but from time to time, I like to throw some things together and
eat something. My favourite things to eat are poutine, pizza and fried chicken. Not the healthiest of
foods, but I enjoy eating them the most.

Personality and Taste

I think I would describe myself as a good mix of an introvert and an extrovert. When I'm around
people I know, I'm usually a really outgoing person and I try to have a really fun, exciting vibe.
If it's people I'm new to, I won't say I'm really dry but I just won't talk as much. I would say I am
pretty hard working as well. I like to get tasks done as soon as possible and I strive to be better person
each day. I also like to try new things. Food, sports, music, even school work, I like to explore and find what
I like. The music that I enjoy listening to is from artists like the late Pop Smoke or Lil Mosey. Basically rap
music, but at the same time I like listening to more relaxed and RnB music such as the Minecraft sound
track on Spotify, Drake, or PnB Rock at times. Their music is something I enjoy listening to time to time.
So you can say my taste in music is pretty weird but, I like what I like. I never really got into watching shows
either, so I can't really speak on what I like to watch. I really like Disney movies and animated stuff though.
I especially like The Lion King and the Cars movies. Shrek is also one of my all time favourites as well.
