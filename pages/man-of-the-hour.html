Who is The Man of The Hour?

Interests

I usually never play alone though. I have three of my best friends hop on with me and we play for hours. The two games I play the most are Forza and Call of Duty Warzone. Forza is really cool because the graphics are really nice and I like racing games sometimes. It's not something I play all the time, but I play Warzone with my friends a lot. Even if we're not the best at the game, we still have a great time and there's never a dull moment. I can talk for hours about my interests but lastly, I am going to talk about my interest in food. I think food is a really cool thing. A bunch of ingredients can make such delicious things. I don't cook, but from time to time, I like to throw some things together and eat something. My favourite things to eat are poutine, pizza and fried chicken. Not the healthiest of foods, but I enjoy eating them the most.
